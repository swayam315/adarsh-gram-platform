How Household Survey Works

Data Collection

Collect household data including:

  • Family member details
  • Basic requirements and needs
  • Infrastructure issues
  • Employment status
  • Education needs

Excel Format

Your Excel file should include columns:

  • SL No. - Serial Number
  • Name - Head of Household
  • Requirement - Specific need
  • Phone Number - Contact details
  • Priority - Urgency level

AI Analysis

Our system will analyze the data to identify:

  • Most common requirements
  • Urgent community needs
  • Priority areas for development
  • Patterns and trends

"Community voices matter. Every household survey brings us closer to understanding the real needs of our village and building a better future together."
